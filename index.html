Our Story

Why Every Mug Matters

In a world that constantly tells you to hustle harder, we're here to remind you: you're already enough.

I'm Anne. After years in school operations—building systems, understanding people, creating environments that work—I realized my heart was pulling me somewhere else. Toward creating. Toward design. Toward pieces that speak to the soul.

Kenyan Feels started with a simple question: What if the mug you drink from every morning could change your day?

These aren't just mugs. They're daily affirmations in your hand. Kenyan pride on your desk. Conversation starters over chai. Reminders that you matter.

Every design is original. Every mug is made in Nairobi. Every purchase supports a creative ecosystem that believes Kenya has stories worth telling.

Whether you're buying for yourself or gifting someone who needs to hear they're loved, valued, or simply enough—welcome to Kenyan Feels.

Let's make your everyday a little more extraordinary.

Anne

Founder & Creative Director, Nairobi
